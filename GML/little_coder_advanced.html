Syllabus

I am a video creator

Create your own videos together. In this module, we discuss the screen recording application Fluid.We should probably alter it after we've recorded it. We will use the Kinemaster app to upload our collected footage and make entertaining HD videos. Let's talk about it in this module as well.

I am an AI programmer

In this module we will discuss Artificial intelligence using Pictoblox. Children will learn to Identify problems where artificial intelligence techniques are applicable. Participate in the design of systems that act intelligently and learn from experience.

I am an Animator

Animation is fun!!!!The use of animation in education is increasing in popularity with new trends in education. In this Module will learn how to make Animated games Using Tynker.

I am a Robotics programmer

Vex Robotics is a well-known robotics curriculum for children. It offers a hands-on, engaging learning experience to assist children in developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaborative skills.

I am an mobile app developer

This module consists of code.org App Lab programs.Let’s convert our game into a mobile application.With code.org, children will learn how to operate a mobile app.

I am a game developer

Game Lab is a programming environment where you can make simple animations and games with objects and characters that interact with each other.
