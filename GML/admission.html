HINDUSTAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY & SCIENCE (HITS)

HINDUSTAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY & SCIENCE (HITS) is India’s most prestigious and recognized institution located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1985, the university offers a variety of undergraduate, graduate, diploma, research and doctoral programs in various disciplines including engineering, technology, architecture, business, law, fashion design, aviation, applied sciences, related sciences, arts and sciences. . HITS was accredited as a UGC University in 2008 and was awarded a Class II rating by the MHRD, granting HITS academic autonomy. Technical courses at UG and PG levels are accredited by AICTE. The NBA has accredited five undergraduate programs offered in Aeronautical Engineering, Automotive Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering as Tier 1 categories. HITS is part of Hindustan’s most prestigious group of institutions catering to the academic needs of over 15,000 students.
