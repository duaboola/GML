What is Career Assessment ?

A career assessment is a way to learn about career options that may be a match for your knowledge, interests, and values. There are different types of assessments that can help you determine which types of careers may intrigue you — and which don’t.

Utmost assessment tools ask questions about what you like, what you don’t like, the type of work terrain you’re comfortable in, and how your personality meshes with different jobs, and also suggest careers that are a match. Career assessment tests won’t give you a definitive answer on what the stylish job for you is, but they will give you options to explore and can help you narrow down your career interests.
